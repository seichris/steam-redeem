# Sample Letter Before Action (template)

Not legal advice. This is a drafting template with placeholders for user-provided facts and jurisdiction-specific references.

**[CLAIMANT NAME]**  
**[CLAIMANT ADDRESS]**  
**[CLAIMANT EMAIL]**  
**[CLAIMANT PHONE (optional)]**

**[DATE]**

**To:**  
**[PUBLISHER / TRADER LEGAL NAME]**  
**[PUBLISHER ADDRESS]**

## Letter Before Action — Digital content non-conformity / misleading marketing

### 1) Parties
I am **[CLAIMANT NAME]** (“the consumer”). You are **[PUBLISHER/TRADER NAME]** (“the trader”).

### 2) Product
- Game: **[GAME TITLE]** (Steam AppID **[APPID]**)  
- Purchase date: **[PURCHASE DATE or “unknown”]**  
- Price paid (or estimated): **[PRICE]**  
- Platform: Steam

### 3) Summary of the dispute
I purchased the above digital content relying on your marketing and product descriptions, including (non-exhaustive):
- **[PROMISE #1]** (Source: **[URL]**, dated **[DATE]**, Evidence ID **[EVID-001]**)  
- **[PROMISE #2]** (Source: **[URL]**, dated **[DATE]**, Evidence ID **[EVID-002]**)  

The product I received is not in conformity with those representations and/or is otherwise defective, including:
- **[DEFECT #1]** (Proof: **[SCREENSHOT/VIDEO/LOG]**, Evidence ID **[EVID-101]**)  
- **[DEFECT #2]** (Proof: **[SCREENSHOT/VIDEO/LOG]**, Evidence ID **[EVID-102]**)  

My request for a refund was denied on **[DATE]** (Evidence ID **[EVID-201]**).

### 4) Legal basis (examples — must be jurisdiction-tailored)
**EU (Directive (EU) 2019/770)**  
Where applicable, the consumer may be entitled to remedies for lack of conformity, including bringing the digital content into conformity, price reduction, or termination. References: Article 14 (and related provisions).

**UK (Consumer Rights Act 2015 — digital content)**  
Where applicable, the consumer may be entitled to repair/replacement and/or price reduction where digital content does not conform to contract, and related remedies.

### 5) Remedy demanded
Within **14 days** of the date of this letter, I require:
1. A full refund of **[AMOUNT]** to the original payment method; and
2. Written confirmation by email to **[EMAIL]**.

If you dispute this, provide a written response within 14 days setting out your position and enclosing any supporting documents.

### 6) Next steps if you do not respond
If I do not receive an adequate response within 14 days, I intend to commence proceedings in the appropriate small-claims process without further notice. I will also seek recovery of allowable fees and costs.

Attached / enclosed:

- **EVID-101** — [description]  
- **EVID-201** — [description]  

Yours faithfully,  

**[CLAIMANT NAME]**
