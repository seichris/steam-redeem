# Disclaimer text inventory (every page, plus key moments)

Not legal advice. Get counsel review before public launch.

## Global (banner on every page)
- “We are not lawyers. We do not provide legal advice.”
- “This is a document-generation and evidence-organization tool only.”
- “You file in your own name. We do not represent you.”
- “We cannot file claims, serve documents, or sign on your behalf.”
- “No outcome is guaranteed.”

## Landing page / marketing
- “For consumer dispute documentation. Not legal advice.”
- “Examples of laws/jurisdictions are informational; applicability depends on your facts.”
- “Do not use this service for harassment, threats, or fraudulent claims.”

## Jurisdiction selection step
- “You are choosing the jurisdiction/court/process; the tool does not decide this for you.”
- UK: “MVP supports England & Wales only (MCOL/OCMC). Scotland/Northern Ireland not yet supported.”
- EU: “Domestic procedures vary by Member State; some flows may provide instructions rather than forms.”
- US: “Small claims is state-specific; US support (if any) will be state-by-state.”

## Uploads (screenshots / documents)
- “Do not upload content you don’t have the right to use.”
- “You are responsible for accuracy and completeness of uploads.”
- “Uploads may contain personal data; see privacy policy.”

## Before payment (Stripe Checkout)
- “Fee is for document-generation services only; not contingent on outcome.”
- “No refunds for court fees paid to third parties.”

## Before generating Letter Before Action
- “Draft for your review. You must verify all facts and citations.”
- “Send method is your responsibility (postal/email) unless you opt into a third-party mailing integration.”

## Before generating court filing packet
- “Not filed by us. You must file/serve/pay fees yourself.”
- “Court rules vary; verify filing requirements with the court.”

## On the generated PDFs
- “Generated with user-provided information; review carefully.”
- “No legal advice; consult a lawyer if unsure.”
